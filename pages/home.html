About Me

I am an postdoctoral researcher at the University of Adelaide, Australia.
I research the wildlife trade and its intersection with conservation and biological invasions. I specialize in using the Internet to gather data on the wildlife trade of live animals.
I earned my PhD at Rutgers University in the Lockwood Lab.
